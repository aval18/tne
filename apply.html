Application
Requirements Please make sure these requirements apply to you before applying! 1. You are 14-30 years in age 2. You are an entrepreneur and have a business - any stage of business is welcome! Whether it is in the inital stages or a full grown operation! 3. You are ready to commit yourself to a few online meetings throughout the year and become an active member! Process & Acceptance To apply, please complete the form below! The process will include a few questions first about you, and then about your passion for entrepreneurship. We will be asking for links for your small business (website, etsy, social media, etc.), but not to worry if you don't have all of these! We will support you at any stage of your business - whether it is in the initial stages or a fully operating business! Regarding acceptance, we will not look at how fully grown your business is (we all start somewhere!), but moreso on how well your business would fit in with the other members and group. There will most likely be a balance of businesses in development level. No Fees There are no fees to join The Next Exec! Free to joing for all :) We will also be offering merch to accepted applicants that is open to purchase.
